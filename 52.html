方・かた (B. 183)

Spoiler English example sentences
A noun forming suffix that indicates a way or a manner in which one does something.
A way of; a manner of; how to

Example sentences

(ks).　
ケーキの作り方を教えて下さい。
Please show me how to make a cake.
(a).　
この漢字の書き方が分かりません。
I don't know how to write this kanji.
(b).　
あの人の歩き方は面白いですね。
His manner of walking is amusing, isn't it?
(c).　
日本語の勉強の仕方を教えて下さい。
Please teach me how to study Japanese language.

Formation

Vます 方
