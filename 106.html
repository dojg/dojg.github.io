難い・にくい (B. 307)

Spoiler English example sentences
Something or someone is hard to ~.
Hard to ~; difficult to ~; don’t do something easily; not readily; not prone to ~

Example sentences

(ks).　
この本は大変読みにくい・にくいです。
This book is very hard to read.
(a).　
このドアは開きにくいですね。
This door doesn't open easily, does it?
(b).　
あの人の名前は覚えにくい。
His name is hard to remember.
(c).　
あの先生は話しにくいです。
That teacher is hard to talk to.
(d).　
この靴は走りにくいです。
These shoes are hard to run in.
