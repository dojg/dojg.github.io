多い・おおい (B. 354)

Spoiler English example sentences
(of quantity or number) a lot
Many; a lot of; much

Example sentences

(ks).　
日本（に）は大学が多い・多いです。
In Japan universities are many.
(a).　
京都（に）はお寺が多いです。
There are many temples in Kyoto.
(b).　
ロスさんの作文（に）は間違いが多いです。
There are many mistakes in Mr. Ross's compositions.
(c).　
一月（に）は雪が多い。
There is a lot of snow in January.
