ね (B. 286)

Spoiler English example sentences
A sentence final particle that indicates the speaker’s request for confirmation or agreement from the hearer about some shared knowledge.
English tag question (such as isn’t it?; is it?; don’t you?; do you?); you know

Example sentences

(ks).　
坂本さんは煙草を吸わない・吸いませんね。
Mr. Sakamoto doesn't smoke, does he? / Mr. Sakamoto, you don't  smoke, do you?
(a).　
A:今日はいい天気ですね。 B:本当にそうですね。
A: Today is a fine day, isn't it?   B: Isn't it!
(b).　
あなたは学生ですね。
You are a student, aren't you?
(c).　
パーティーにいらっしゃいますね。
You are going to the party, aren't you?
