に (5) (B. 297)

Spoiler English example sentences
A particle which indicates purpose when someone moves from one place to another.
To do something; in order to do something

Example sentences

(ks).　
私はデパートへ贈り物を買いに行った・行きました。
I went to a department store to buy a gift
(a).　
学生が質問をしに来た。
A student came to ask questions.
(b).　
そこへ何をしに行くんですか。
To do what are you going there? / For what are you going there?
(c).　
村井さんは昼ご飯を食べにうちへ帰った。
Mr. Murai went home to eat his lunch.
