欲しい・ほしい (2) (B. 146)

Spoiler English example sentences
Want someone (who is not higher in status than the speaker) to do something.
Want (someone) to do (something)

Example sentences

(ks).　
私はあなたに英語を教えて欲しい・欲しいです。
I want you to teach me English.
(a).　
私は子供達に私と一緒に住んで欲しい。
I want my children to live together with me.
(b).　
あなたは誰に来て欲しいですか。
Who do you want to come?

Formation

Vて ほしい  
  話してほしい Want (someone) to talk
  食べてほしい Want (someone) to eat
